TrivAI (Trivia Mode) Details

TrivAI challenges you with a fresh set of AI-generated trivia questions every day. Topics span general knowledge, history, science, pop culture, and more. Track your score, improve over time, and compete with friends on the daily leaderboard. Whether you have a minute or ten, TrivAI is perfect for a quick mental workout and discovering fun facts!

Escape Room Game Details

In the Escape Room Game mode, players navigate a virtual room filled with puzzles, clues, and riddles. Your objective is to uncover hidden objects, solve logic challenges, and ultimately "escape" within a set time limit. Each room presents unique themes and difficulty levels—ranging from ancient temples to futuristic labs. Collaboration options allow multiple players to work together or compete for the fastest escape time!

Puzzle Mode offers a variety of classic and new puzzles for solo enjoyment. Choose from crosswords, word searches, Sudoku, and logic challenges that adapt to your skill level. Spot the AI Details

In Spot the AI, you'll be shown image pairs—one real photo and one AI-generated—and you decide which is which. Each round increases in difficulty as AI models become more convincing. Hone your visual detection skills, learn about the subtle differences between authentic and synthetic images, and compete on leaderboards to see who can spot the AI most accurately!
